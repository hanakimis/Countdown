Handoff notes — Countdown styles
SHARED
· data: days / hr / min / sec from Calendar.dateComponents (existing)
· tick geometry: TickDialView — width 2.3pt, lengthRatio 0.24, ringScale 0.95
· concentric bands: band 0.08 × size, gap 0.032 × size, outer r 0.475 × size
· style persisted: UserDefaults key "visualStyle" ∈ ledger | editorial | tminus
· settings: one sheet — Style · Date · Refill (fixed: launch volley)
LAUNCH-VOLLEY REFILL (all styles)
· trigger: unit hits 0 → parent unit −1, child ring refills
· per-tick delay: hash(i) × 420 ms (stable pseudo-random, sin-hash)
· per-tick travel: 340 ms, easeOutBack (c1 = 1.70158), radius 0 → slot
· color: accent @ 30% → 100% during travel, settles to filled gray
· hour rollover: min + sec rings volley simultaneously (double eject)
STYLE 1 · LEDGER (7a)
· bg #212836 · accent #BFE8FB · ticks rgba(151,151,151,.9) / .14 track
· numerals Georgia/New York serif · rows sec→min→hr, 56pt mini dials · dot ledger 16 col

· bg #F5F2EC · ink #1E1D1B · accent #4C6CA8
